Frequently Asked Questions

Who is a right Physiotheraphist for me?

PhysioPlus tailors physiotherapy care to each patient's unique needs. Whether it's post-surgery, sports injuries, or unclear symptoms, we match you with the right specialist for effective treatment.

How is sports physiotherapy different from physiotherapy?

"At PhysioPlus, we provide holistic sports physiotherapy, addressing the root causes of pain and limitations. We stay with you to ensure lasting results and refer you to specialists when necessary.

How Physio's are assigned by Physioplus?

"At PhysioPlus, we match you with experienced physiotherapists based on your condition, location, and any gender preferences. Our aim is to provide convenient and effective treatment with the right physiotherapist for your needs.

What procedures are followed in physiotherapy?

At PhysioPlus, our physiotherapists assess each patient to create a tailored treatment plan. We use various approaches, including manual exercises, soft tissue mobilization, electrotherapy, heat therapy, taping for sports injuries, and ultrasound, based on individual needs and conditions.

Do I get relief from pain by performing the RICE (REST-ICE-COMPRESS-ELEVATION) procedure?

Yes, you will surely get relief but not be able to treat the source of your pain. To cure the source of pain, you need to consult a specialised physiotherapist to restore proper mobility by performing the right exercise routines prescribed by physio. And Physioplus helps you to find the best physio in town.

Can I trust the Physio's from PhysioPlus?

We have a thorough verification process for every physio on Physioplus. Any physiotherapist that you consult with is a verified medical practitioner, with all their qualification documents manually and electronically verified by our team. We also take feedback from patients to ensure that Physio maintains high standards for online consultations.
