Introduction


We proudly introduce our groundbreaking mobile application, an innovative platform designed to seamlessly connect athletes with skilled physiotherapists in a manner that is efficient, authentic, and expedient. Bid farewell to protracted wait times and uncertain referrals; our application facilitates precise matches between athletes and certified physiotherapy experts who possess a specialization in sports-related injuries and recuperation. By harnessing the power of intuitive technology, our application streamlines the process of booking appointments, accessing bespoke treatment strategies, and monitoring personal progress. This entire suite of services is conveniently accessible through the user's mobile device, underscoring our commitment to convenience and accessibility. Catering to a diverse spectrum of athletes, ranging from burgeoning enthusiasts to seasoned professionals, our application serves as the quintessential solution for prompt and dependable physiotherapeutic care. Furthermore, in line with our unwavering dedication to elevating sports-related healthcare, we are diligently establishing a network of accessible and cost-effective sports physiotherapy clinics across India. Our visionary initiative epitomizes a comprehensive and holistic approach to addressing the myriad needs of athletes, encapsulating the essence of a singular, all-encompassing solution aptly coined, "One Stop Smart Solution for the Game."

Why we started?

In 2019, one of our co-founders, Hitendra, participated in the prestigious Senior Men’s National Football Championship, known as "The Santosh Trophy," held in Goa. Unfortunately, he sustained an injury during the championship, which significantly impacted his well-being. Upon returning to Jaipur, he sought medical assistance from multiple healthcare professionals but did not experience the desired relief. Consequently, his ability to focus on training and perform optimally was compromised. Recognizing the critical need for comprehensive sports-related medical support, Hitendra engaged in a conversation with his co-founder, Jaswant. Drawing from his experience and insights, Jaswant suggested seeking the expertise of a sports physiotherapist. This recommendation led to a breakthrough, as Hitendra's condition improved significantly under the guidance of the sports physiotherapist. Motivated by this transformative experience, Hitendra and Jaswant embarked on a mission to establish a holistic solution catering to athletes' multifaceted requirements. Through meticulous research and a shared vision, they conceptualized and subsequently launched PHYSIOPLUS in 2022.

PHYSIOPLUS stands as a testament to our commitment to bridging the gap in comprehensive sports-related healthcare, offering athletes a singular platform for accessing specialized medical attention, optimizing their training regimens, and ultimately enhancing their performance on the field. Our journey is rooted in a profound understanding of the challenges athletes face, and our purpose is to empower them with the tools and support necessary to excel in their respective disciplines.
