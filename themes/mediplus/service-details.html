Physiotherapy is a specialized field of healthcare dedicated to optimizing physical health and well-being. It focuses on assessing, diagnosing, and treating a wide range of musculoskeletal and neurological conditions that affect an individual's physical function and mobility. Physiotherapists employ various techniques, including exercises, manual therapy, and modalities like ultrasound and electrical stimulation, to alleviate pain, improve muscle strength, enhance flexibility, and restore overall physical function. Whether it's rehabilitating an injury, managing chronic pain, or helping individuals recover from surgery, physiotherapy plays a crucial role in promoting and maintaining physical health, enabling people to lead active and fulfilling lives.
