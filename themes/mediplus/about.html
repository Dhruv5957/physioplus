Our Mission

PhysioPlus has introduced a comprehensive assessment tailored for sports players with a primary focus on injury prevention. Within this assessment framework, a dedicated team of professionals, including sports physiotherapists, sports psychologists, and various other specialists.

Our Planning

This encompassing evaluation addresses critical injury-related concerns, culminating in the creation of a comprehensive assessment report. This report gauges the likelihood of an athlete encountering injuries in the near future. Utilizing this assessment report, we are equipped to proactively intervene.

Our Vision

Our overarching objective in implementing this protocol is to diminish the incidence of player injuries, consequently fostering the growth of the sport. As the prevalence of player injuries subsides, a concurrent increase in the population of well-conditioned athletes is anticipated.
